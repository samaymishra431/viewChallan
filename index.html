Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the challan system.

How do I check my pending challans? expand_more
Simply enter your vehicle registration number in the search bar on our homepage. We will fetch the latest data from the central traffic database.
Is the payment process secure? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We do not store your banking details.
Can I contest a challan? expand_more
Yes. If you believe a challan has been issued incorrectly, you can use our 'Contest' feature to connect with legal experts.
How quickly is the status updated? expand_more
Status updates are typically instant. In rare cases, government servers may take up to 24 hours to reflect the payment.
Do I need to register to check fines? expand_more
No, you can check fines as a guest. However, registering allows you to receive automated alerts for new challans.
What payment methods are accepted? expand_more
We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular mobile wallets for your convenience.
Can I pay fines for multiple vehicles? expand_more
Yes, registered users can add multiple vehicles to their dashboard and manage payments for all of them from a single account.
Is there a service fee for payments? expand_more
We charge a nominal convenience fee to maintain our platform and provide secure transaction services. The exact amount is displayed before payment.
How do I get a receipt? expand_more
A digital receipt is automatically sent to your registered email address immediately after a successful payment. You can also download it from your dashboard.
What if my payment fails but money is deducted? expand_more
If a transaction fails, the deducted amount is usually refunded to the source account within 5-7 business days automatically. Contact support if issues persist.
