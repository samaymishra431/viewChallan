Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check my pending challans? expand_more
You can check your pending challans by navigating to the "Challans" tab in the sidebar. Alternatively, use the Quick Pay search on your dashboard by entering your vehicle number.
What payment methods are accepted? expand_more
We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking from major banks, and UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm).
I paid my challan but the status is still 'Pending'. expand_more
Usually, the status updates instantly. However, in some cases due to bank server delays, it may take up to 24 hours. If it persists, please use the contact form on this page with your transaction ID.
How do I contest a challan in court? expand_more
If you believe a challan was issued in error, you can choose the 'Contest in Court' option on the challan detail page. This will forward your case to the Virtual Traffic Court. You may need to appear virtually or physically as per summons.
Can I download a receipt for past payments? expand_more
Yes, go to 'Payment History' in the sidebar to view and download receipts for all your settled challans.
